WP After Payment is a lightweight WordPress plugin designed to handle post-payment actions and workflows with simplicity and flexibility. It allows developers and website owners to define custom behaviors that are triggered after a successful transaction, making it easier to automate processes such as redirects, confirmations, notifications, or content access.

Built with a focus on performance and ease of integration, this plugin works seamlessly within the WordPress ecosystem and can be extended to support various payment gateways or custom checkout systems. Whether you're running a small digital product site, a service-based business, or experimenting with custom payment flows, WP After Payment provides a clean and developer-friendly foundation.

Key features include:

Custom actions after successful payments
Easy integration with existing WordPress setups
Flexible and extendable architecture
Lightweight and performance-oriented design

WP After Payment is ideal for developers who want full control over what happens after a payment is completed, without relying on heavy or complex eCommerce solutions.
